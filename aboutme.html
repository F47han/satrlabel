Our Story

Satr was born from a deeply personal place — a desire to create abayas that honour identity while embracing modern elegance. Each piece is crafted with the care you'd want for yourself and the women you love. This isn't just clothing; it's a celebration of the confident, graceful woman you are.

Our Commitment

At Satr, we believe modest fashion should be both beautiful and meaningful. Every abaya is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted to honour tradition while embracing contemporary elegance. The name Satr — meaning modesty and covering in Arabic — sits at the heart of everything we create.

Our fabrics are carefully selected for their drape, comfort, and durability, because you deserve pieces that feel as beautiful as they look. From the hand-applied lace on the Flora to the flowing silhouette of the Sahara, every detail is considered and finished with care. When you wear Satr, you carry a piece of artistry and intention.

Our Policies

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. We only collect the information needed to process your order and provide a smooth shopping experience.

Your details are kept secure and never shared with anyone except trusted partners such as payment providers and delivery services.

Refund & Returns

If your Satr piece isn't right, you may request a return or exchange within 14 days of receiving your order.

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and returned with all original tags intact. Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed promptly.

We are unable to accept returns for items showing signs of wear, damage, or fragrance.

Shipping

Orders are prepared within 1–3 working days. You will receive tracking details once your order is on its way.

We offer free shipping on all UK orders. We are not responsible for courier delays beyond our control.

International Orders — We are currently unable to process international orders directly through our website. If you are based outside the UK and would like to purchase an abaya, please reach out to us via Instagram or email and we will be happy to assist you personally.
