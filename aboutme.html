Our Story

As the founder of Satr, I created this brand from a deeply personal need — to find abayas that honour my identity while embracing modern elegance. Each piece is crafted with the care I'd want for my own daughters, sisters, and friends. This isn't just clothing; it's a celebration of the confident, graceful woman you are.

Our Commitment

At Satr, we believe that modest fashion should be both beautiful and meaningful. Every abaya is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted to honour tradition while embracing contemporary elegance. We work with skilled artisans who share our passion for quality and attention to detail.

Our fabrics are carefully selected for their drape, comfort, and durability — because you deserve pieces that feel as beautiful as they look. When you wear Satr, you're not just wearing an abaya; you're carrying a piece of artistry and intention.

Our Policies

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. We only collect the information needed to process your order and provide a smooth shopping experience.

Your details are kept secure and never shared with anyone except trusted partners such as payment providers and delivery services.

Refund & Returns

If your Satr piece isn't right, you can request a return or exchange within 14 days of receiving it.

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and returned with all original tags. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed.

We cannot accept returns for items that show signs of wear, damage, or perfume.

Shipping Policy

Orders are prepared within 1–3 working days. You'll receive tracking details once your order is on its way.

We are not responsible for courier delays or customs clearance issues beyond our control.

International Orders

Free shipping within the UK. For international orders, please message us on Instagram @satr.labell before placing your order.
